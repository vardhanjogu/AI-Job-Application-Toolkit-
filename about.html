About the AI Job Application Toolkit

Stop wasting time on generic applications. Get hired faster with precision-guided AI.

Our Mission

We built this toolkit to eliminate the most tedious and time-consuming parts of the job search. Instead of spending hours tailoring a cover letter or guessing what interview questions might be asked, our tool uses advanced AI (the Gemini API) to cross-reference your experience against specific job requirements instantly.

Our goal is to make every application you submit targeted, professional, and impactful, giving you the competitive edge you need.

How the 7 Tools Work

All 7 functions are powered by the same simple principle: we compare the **Job Description (JD)** with your **Full Resume Text** to find the exact match points and gaps. This is the power of prompt engineering.

  1. Cover Letter Generation: The AI analyzes the JD for required skills and then searches your Resume for corresponding keywords, projects, or accomplishments, weaving them into a persuasive, custom-written draft.
  2. Skill Extraction & Role Mission Summary: These tools rapidly process the JD to give you the 8 most critical skills to focus on, as well as a one-paragraph summary of the role's core mission, ensuring you instantly understand the job's true priority.
  3. Resume Gap Identification: This is a powerful self-critique tool. The AI points out 3-4 specific requirements from the JD that are not clearly met by your resume, allowing you to prepare defenses or clarify experience *before* the interview.
  4. Interview Questions: Generates 5 unique, highly relevant questions by comparing what the JD needs with what your resume claims, forcing you to prepare specific, difficult-to-answer questions.
  5. Draft STAR Response Idea: Provides a structured template (Situation, Task, Action, Result) based on your resume's potential match points, giving you a ready-to-use behavioral answer template for interview practice.
  6. Draft Follow-up Email: Drafts a thank-you email template that strategically incorporates specific references to both the JD and your resume, making the follow-up personal and impactful.

Data Safety and Privacy

Your privacy is paramount. We do not store your Job Description or Resume text. All data is passed directly to the Gemini API for ephemeral processing and is not saved on our servers. The entire application is hosted statically by Netlify.
