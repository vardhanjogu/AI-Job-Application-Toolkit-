About the AI Job Application Toolkit

Stop wasting time on generic applications. Get hired faster with precision-guided AI.

Our Mission

We built this toolkit to eliminate the most tedious and time-consuming parts of the job search. Instead of spending hours tailoring a cover letter or guessing what interview questions might be asked, our tool uses advanced AI (the Gemini API) to cross-reference your experience against specific job requirements instantly.

Our goal is to make every application you submit targeted, professional, and impactful, giving you the competitive edge you need.

How the 7 Tools Work

All 7 functions are powered by the same simple principle: we compare the **Job Description (JD)** with your **Full Resume Text** to find the exact match points and gaps.

1. Generate Cover Letter

The AI analyzes the JD for required skills and then searches your Resume for corresponding keywords, projects, or accomplishments, weaving them into a persuasive, custom-written draft.

2. Analyze JD & Extract Skills

Processes the JD instantly to identify the **8 most critical skills and keywords** you must use to get past Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

3. Summarize Role Mission

Provides a concise, single-paragraph summary of the role's core mission, the main challenges, and the expected impact, helping you prepare your elevator pitch.

4. Identify Resume Gaps

A powerful self-critique tool. The AI points out **3-4 specific requirements** from the JD that are not clearly met by your resume, allowing you to prepare defenses or clarify experience *before* the interview.

5. Generate Interview Questions

Generates **5 unique, highly relevant questions** by comparing the JD's needs against your resume's claims, forcing you to practice specific, difficult-to-answer situational questions.

6. Draft STAR Response Idea

Provides a structured template (**Situation, Task, Action, Result**) based on a critical JD requirement and your resume, giving you a ready-to-use behavioral answer template for interview practice.

7. Draft Follow-up Email

Drafts a professional thank-you email template that strategically incorporates specific references to both the JD and your resume, making the follow-up personal and impactful.

Data Safety and Privacy

Your privacy is paramount. We do not store your Job Description or Resume text. All data is passed directly to the Gemini API for ephemeral processing and is not saved on our servers. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details.
